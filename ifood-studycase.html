This is a design for a Web plataform of Online travel and tourism System in Sri-Lanka .

Intro

A travel planning platform that offers hotel, transportation, and guide booking services to Sri Lankan travelers.AI-based travel recommendation system using.

Objective

To create an AI recommendation system that can evaluate user preferences and behavior in relation to travel (using AI chatbot).
To provide a user-friendly interface where customers may enter their trip choices and spending limits.
To create customized recommendations, collect and evaluate information on a range of trip locations, places to stay and activities.
To continuously enhance the recommendation system based on user feedback and interactions.
To provide seamless communication with backend systems that manage travel-related service booking, pricing, availability, and inventory.
To implement strong security and privacy safeguards to safeguard user information.

The Problem

This project aims to simplify customized travel planning, addressing the limitations of generic travel recommendations that fail to match individual preferences and budgets. Existing platforms often overlook personalized needs, making trip planning time-consuming and complex. Challenges include selecting destinations, lodging, and activities, with current technologies lacking seamless integration of bookings, costs, and availability. This leads to disjointed and inefficient travel experiences, resulting in wasted time and money for travelers.

The Solution

The introduction highlights the need for a modern AI-powered travel and tourism system to provide personalized experiences, addressing the limitations of traditional travel services. It emphasizes improving customer satisfaction and transforming travel planning by defining the goals and motivations for developing the proposed solution.
