This is a design for a Web plataform of Taxi Reservation system.

Intro

The City Taxi (PVT) Ltd project aims to develop a modern, web-based taxi reservation system to meet rising consumer expectations and technological advancements. Targeting lower to high-class clients, the system prioritizes economy, reliability, and ease of use over traditional taxi services.

Objective

• Creating a website with a reservation system so that travelers can book taxis with ease.
• Setting in place rules for passenger and driver registration.
• Enabling drivers to update their status as “Available” or “Busy” and enabling real-time tracking of their availability.
• Notifying passengers via SMS when their reservations are confirmed, along with the driver and vehicle information.
• Processing trip payments automatically and informing drivers and passengers with their confirmations.
• Including a system for drivers to be rated according to their experiences by passengers.

The Problem

Traditional taxi reservations face inefficiencies, transparency issues, and security concerns. Customers experience long wait times, difficulty finding drivers, and fare uncertainty, while manual booking leads to miscommunication and scheduling conflicts. Drivers struggle with disorganized trip assignments and unpredictable income due to the lack of a centralized system. City Taxi (PVT) Ltd aims to solve these challenges by developing a web-based taxi reservation system that enhances efficiency, reliability, and security through modern technology.

The Solution

The City Taxi (PVT) Ltd web-based system aims to enhance taxi reservations by addressing current inefficiencies through modern technology. It offers online registration for drivers and passengers, providing login credentials via email for easy access. Passengers can book taxis online and receive instant SMS confirmations with driver and vehicle details, ensuring security. The system integrates real-time location tracking and driver status updates ("AVAILABLE" or "BUSY") to optimize ride assignments and reduce wait times. Additional features include a passenger rating system to maintain service quality, manual booking via phone for non-registered users, automatic fare calculation, and online payment processing for seamless transactions.
